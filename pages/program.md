## Conference Overview

EMO 2027 spans four days with the following structure:

- **Saturday, April 5**: Tutorial Day + Welcome Reception
- **Sunday, April 6**: Conference Day 1 (Opening, Keynote, Paper Sessions)
- **Monday, April 7**: Conference Day 2 (Keynote, Sessions, Boat Tour, Banquet)
- **Tuesday, April 8**: Conference Day 3 (Keynote, Sessions, Awards, Closing)
